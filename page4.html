Becoming a member of the CIW provides a lot of advantages and opportunities. And main advantage is that you become part of finding the solutions that are affecting millions of Youth across Zambia and the world at large.
  • Self-Satisfaction: Yeah, that’s what makes our life exciting
  • Community Participation: Make a difference! Become an integral part of the global youth movement.
  • Leadership Development: Through work experience, the CIW offers mentoring and leadership training for personal development. 
  • Network: Your professional and personal network will be expanded since you’ll find many new people working along with you.
  • Improves Your Profile: In future, if you would like to apply for some higher study programs like MBA or Job, you can highlight your work with the CIW.
  • Personal Development: The important factor that stands you out from a pile of normal people is your communication along with leadership skills. CIW is working to improve your communications and shape your leadership skills.
  • Resources Center: The CIW Resources Center provides information on the most updated youth insights, solutions, and relevant resources. CIW brings you closer to various opportunities such as scholarship opportunities and many more.

MEMBERSHIP FEE

In order to support our Non Profit in its daily operations, new members are requested to pay a no-refundable fee of K200 and a K150 there after annual membership fee.
